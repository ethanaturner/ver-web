The Problem

Remember that movie you used to love ten years ago and want to watch now? Love that TV show but don't subscribe to cable? We feel your frustration . . .

The Solution

Enter Ver. With Ver (Spanish for "to watch" or "to see") you can simply enter the name of your show or movie, search through the filtered results from a massive database, then see if it's available on the services you subscribe to or if you can watch it for free. So sit back, and let the watching begin.
